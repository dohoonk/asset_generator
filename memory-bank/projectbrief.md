## Project Brief

- **Name:** Anime Asset Generator
- **Goal:** Provide visual-novel creators with a fast way to generate anime-style assets (characters and backgrounds) and short background music via a web UI backed by Replicate models.
- **Output:** Downloadable images (with optional background removal) and short instrumental tracks suitable for looping.
- **Audience:** Visual Novel / game developers who need quick iteration on art and ambience.
