## Product Context

- **Problem:** Indie VN teams need consistent anime assets and ambience quickly without deep ML knowledge.
- **Solution:** A Next.js UI that wraps curated Replicate models, applies anime-friendly prompt defaults, and lets users generate batches, manage session history, and download results.
- **Users:** Writers/artists prototyping sprites, backgrounds, CGs; need fast turnaround and simple controls.
- **UX Goals:** Clean anime-themed UI, minimal friction (prompt + optional reference), quick defaults for dimensions/batch size, easy history recall, and one-click downloads.
